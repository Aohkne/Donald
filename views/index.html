The Donald Project offers online courses, early intervention therapy, psychological counselling, life skills, and social communication classes for autistic children and their families. It aims to assist them in reaching their full potential, integrating into society, and fostering a supportive environment.

WHAT WE PROVIDE

We help to provide all the necessary services, such as therapy, skills training, psychology counselling, etc. All services are guaranteed.

THERAPY

Various forms of therapy provided, including behavioral, speech, and occupational therapy.

SKILLS TRAINING

Life skills training to help individuals become more independent.

PSYCHOLOGY COUNSELLING

Professional psychological counselling services for individuals and families.

SUPPORT GROUPS

Support groups for families to share experiences and gain support from others.

ABOUT US

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive services for children with autism and their families. Our goal is to support and empower individuals to reach their full potential.
